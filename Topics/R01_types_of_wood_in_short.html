Types of wood in short

This reference describes shortly types of wood which are materials to our furniture production.

Table 1. Types of wood
Wood Picture Properties Particularities Use Structure Occurence
Acacia high strength, hardness, and resistance Insensitive to moisture and fungi. Suitable for indoor and outdoor use. varied smooth grain Europe, Asia, Australia, Africa, America
Old wood Durable and wear-resistant. a mixture of different types of wood Particularly suitable for outdoors. Nice coloring with signs of wear from the weather. non-applicable
Oak hard, heavy, and durable widely spread wood used in a variety of ways. Suitable for indoor and outdoor use. striking natural grain America, Europe, Asia
Mango light, hard, and robust concise grain Unsuitable for outdoor use. dotted, slightly cracked grain Mainly in Asia, but also Europe and America.
Sheesham / Rosewood hard and resistant large and wide tree trunks Rather unsuitable for outdoor use. wavy, round grain native to Asia
