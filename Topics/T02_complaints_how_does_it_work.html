Complaints - how does it work?

What to do if you received damaged item? What options you have when it comes to solution of your claim? Here you will learn the entire process of complaint.

You have finally received your product from Meble kolonialne24, but unfortunately it is damaged? No problem! Our team will take care of your concerns. In the video and study case below we explain how exactly it works.

Important: Our team in the warehouse thoroughly prepars the items to transport. Quality assurance employess are checking each item before shipping to reject furniture damaged during the transport from India.
Remember: When the weight of the package is above 30 kg such delivery is packed on a wooden pallet to protect it from damage during the delivery to your home. Our workers wrapp in bubble foil, protect each corner with sponge protectors and packed in carton every item.

Anna has ordered a chest in our e-shop and chosen a shipping company to deliver it to her home.

  1. At the day of the delivery she has received an sms with the phone number to the driver and the information that he will deliver her the chest today . She has called the courier end established with him the details of the delivery.
  2. The courier has left her the chest on the driveway. Anna has checked the package and found a visible damage. She confirmed the receipt and required to unpack the item in presence of the courier. It appeared that the corner of the chest is crushed so she draw up a statement of damage where she described the flaw and marked all used protections.
    Warning: Missing pallet is also a reason to unpack the item in the presence of the courier.
    Note: If Anna had not found any visible damage of the packege the courier is not oblidged to stay until she will check the item. However, we highly recommend to unpack the item as soon as possible after the delivery and after finding any damages call the courier back and draw up a statement of damage.
  3. Anna has taken the pictures of the damaged corner and send them to our support together with a detail description and a copy of the statement of damage.
  4. Our team has studied the case and decided to give Anna three options how to solve this.
    • a discount or a gift card for future purchase that will compensate Anna's losses
    • restoration of the damaged item or paying for such service done by Anna's trusted carpenter
    • an exchange to a new item
    Remember: As a last resort Anna can give up of the purchase and send the chest back. However, if restoration or exchanging the item is possible, Meble kolonialne 24 are not oblidged to accept such decision as a first option.
  5. Anna has decided to exchange the item for new one. Meble kolonialne 24 has organize the pick up and delivery of the new chest.
Anna has received a new chest and this time nothing wrong has happened with it during the delivery. Anna can now enjoy the special and high-quality furniture for a very long time.
Note: Your most dissatisfied customers are your best source of learning. - Bill Gates
