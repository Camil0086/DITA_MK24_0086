Order procedure

You have lost during the order procedure? How to order unavailable items? Learn details of order procedure and time of delivery.

You can easily order furniture in our online shop by putting them to the shopping cart. Then you can choose the payment and delivery.

If you will put one item into the shopping cart the system will automatically choose the cheapest way of delivery. Below you can see an example of the shopping cart with one item in it.

Beware! The sample price of a delivery may be change.

You can choose the delivery to an InPost parcel locker or a post office / Ruch kiosk / Orlen gas station for selected items below 30 kg weight. In the former you will choose the location of a locker under the options of payment and delivery. In the latter the option to choose the pickup point will appear during the stage of delivery data. Below an examples of shopping carts with an InPost parcel locker and a post office / Ruch kiosk / Orlen gas station chosen as a shipping method.

  1. After selecting the form of payment and shipping choose "PRZEJDŹ DO KASY" to summarize the shopping cart or "KONTYNUUJ ZAKUPY" to continue in online shop.
  2. Choose "DANE OSOBOWE" to continue the purchase or "POWRÓT DO KOSZYKA" to go back. In the former you have to choose if you want to buy the items with or without register.
  3. To buy the items without registration please ignore the registration section and fill in all spaces marked by asterix [*] as obligatory and all others as optional.
  4. To buy the items with registration please fill in the registration section together with all obligatory spaces and optional if you wish.
    Notice: If you have alraeady an account in our e-shop it is enough to log in and all necessary data will fill in automatically.
    Important: If you choose to register your account you will not be able to use the same e-mail address to buy anything else choosing option without registration.
  5. If you have chosen the delivery to a post office / Ruch kiosk / Orlen gas station at the stage of login / filling personal data the map will shop up on which you can choose preferred delivery point.
    Note: Since the map is integrated with the personal data section, after login or filling the spaces the map will show all delivery points in the neighbourhood of the given address.
    Important: If you choose the option of delivery with the payment on delivery [only in case of selected items below 30 kg weight], post offices are the only delivery points available.
  6. As a next step you can add any additional information and choose if you wish to receive the invoice [Proszę o wystawienie faktury (…)]. In the latter an additional section appears with all spaces you need to fill in to receive the invoice. Accepting the Terms and Conditions [Akceptuję regulamin sklepu], and accepting that the order implies the obligation to pay [Rozumiem, że złożenie zamówienia wiąże się z obowiązkiem zapłaty].
  7. If you wish to finish the order select Order and Pay [ZAMÓW I ZAPŁAĆ].
After placing the order you will receive an e-mail with all the details concerning the payment and the delivery.
Note: In our online shop there are three types of item availability. If the item is in our own waerhouses the time of shipping is from 1 to 3 days what is marked by light green colour. If it is available in the warehouses of the importer the time of delivery is from 14 to 31 days. If the item is at the stage of production the time of delivery is from 1 to 3 months in case of items manufactured in Europe and from 3 to 6 months in case of those manufactured in India. SInce the items at the stage of production are not available it is not possible to add them to shopping cart and buy them. THe only way to order such items is to send us an e-mail and we will reply with all the details concerning such order.
Note: If you add to your shopping cart items with delivery time 1-3 days and 14-31 days, we will deliver all items within 14 to 31 days. If wish to order items with delivery time 1-3 days or/and 14-31 days and those at stage of production we are not able to book up the former until the latter will arrive. In such case we have to send the items in two sets.

In case of any doubts or questions please do not hasitate to contact us.
