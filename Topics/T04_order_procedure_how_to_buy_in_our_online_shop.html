Order procedure - how to buy in our online shop

You can easily order furniture in our online shop by putting them to the shopping cart. Then you can choose the payment and delivery.

If you will put one item into the shopping cart the system will automatically choose the cheapest way of delivery. Below you can see an example of the shopping cart with one item in it.

Beware! The sample price of a delivery may be change.

You can choose the delivery to an InPost parcel locker or a post office / Ruch kiosk / Orlen gas station for selected items below 30 kg weight. In the former you will choose the location of a locker under the options of payment and delivery. In the latter the option to choose the pickup point will appear during the stage of delivery data
